Who We Are

Headquartered at 56 Tosi Street, Phuthaditjhaba, Free State, Pahblo Security is a premier protection and security firm. Established by local security experts, we pride ourselves on rapid response capabilities, rigorous tactical training, and uncompromising integrity.
