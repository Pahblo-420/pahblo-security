Who We Are

Founded in 2019 by CEO and Managing Director Paballo D. Lipopo, Pahblo Security is a premier protection and tactical security firm. Headquartered at 56 Tosi Street, Phuthaditjhaba, Free State, we are built by local security experts who pride themselves on rapid response capabilities, rigorous tactical training, and uncompromising integrity.

We believe that safety is a fundamental right. Operating out of our Phuthaditjhaba base, our protective network spans across Kestell, Harrismith, Bethlehem, and surrounding communities. Today, we proudly boast an active roster of over 1,200 secured clients, providing absolute peace of mind to private residences, corporate entities, and high-value assets alike.
